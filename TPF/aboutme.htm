My name is Aria Patel, and I am a senior at Babson College. During my junior year of college, I took the opportunity to study abroad for the entire year. I spent my first semester in Milan, Italy, and my second semester in Edinburgh, Scotland. With my extensive time living abroad, I got to travel to so many different places across the continent, and got a taste of how to travel on a budget. This website is tailored with suggestions that have things to do during the day, cost-effective places to stay, and insider tips! I have also included lots of photos from my time travelling!

I have included two main sections: Itineraries, and Recommendations. In the itineraries section I have provided detailed plans for what to do in each place on each day. These trips span from weekend to week long trips. In the recommendations section, I have included restaurants, activities, bars, etc. with personal reviews on places I have been!
