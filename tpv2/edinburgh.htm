    • Edinburgh Bucket List

    There are so many things to do and see in Edinburgh, despite it being such a small city! It's the most charming place you'll visit.
    Look below for some things that are definitely worth your time!

    • Aruther's Seat
      • Great for hiking, picnics, and watching sunset!
    • Greyfriar Bobby
      • Touch his nose for good luck!
    • Walk in Dean Village
    • Calton Hill
      • Gorgeous viewpoint with a free museum!
    • Ross Fountain
    • Leith & Portobello
      • Different markets throughout the year with a gorgeous waterfront and many restaurants!
    • Stockbridge Market
    • Swanston Farms
      • If you want to go somewhere close by to see the Highland Cows, this is the place!
    • Musselborough Race Course
      • A super fun experience to feel like a true Edi native!
